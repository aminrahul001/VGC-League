The Format

Every roster is built to the exact rules the real VGC circuit is playing under right now, so what you draft is what you'd bring to a Regional.

Battle Structure

Doubles, bring six, pick four

Each match is a 2v2 double battle. Trainers bring a full team of six and reveal four for the game once pairings are set, first two selected lead off.

Mega Evolution

One Mega per battle

Regulation M-B is the second Mega format of Pokémon Champions. Your roster can carry more than one Mega-capable Pokémon, but only one may Mega Evolve in a given game.

Roster Rules

No duplicates, no restrictions

No two Pokémon sharing a Pokédex number, no two holding the same item. No species are restricted this season, the full 208-Pokémon, 76-Mega pool is open to draft.

Match Length

Best-of-three, top cut

Regular season sets are best-of-one to keep the pace up. Playoffs and the season final are played best-of-three, matching Regional and Worlds-level events.

The Pool

22 new arrivals

M-B adds 22 Pokémon and 16 new Mega Evolutions on top of the M-A pool, meaning the draft board is deeper than the season before it.

Season Window

Runs the live regulation

The league is timed to the official M-B ranked window, June 17 through September 2, so your roster stays legal for the entire season, no rule changes mid-draft.

How the Draft Works

One night, one board, no re-picks. Here's the order of operations from sign-up to season one.

01

Build your priority list

Rank the Pokémon you want most from the legal M-B pool before draft night. You won't get to think this hard once the clock starts.

02

Seed gets set

Draft order is randomized and published 48 hours ahead of time, so you know exactly when you're on the clock.

03

Snake draft, live

Picks alternate direction each round. Everyone gets a 90-second clock per pick, same as the in-game team preview timer.

04

Lock in and build

Once your six are drafted, rosters lock. From there it's on you to build sets, EV spreads, and Tera choices before week one.

Season Schedule

Everything's timed to fit inside the current regulation window before the meta rotates again.

Week 0

Draft Night

Live snake draft. All eight teams pick their six-Pokémon rosters on the board.

Weeks 1–6

Regular Season

Single round-robin, best-of-one sets, results and usage stats posted after every week.

Week 7

Playoffs

Top four rosters advance to a best-of-three bracket.

Sep 2

Season Final & Regulation Close

Championship set is played the same week Regulation Set M-B rotates out, so the whole season lives inside one format.

Claim a Slot

Eight slots per season, first come first drafted. You'll get your seed and draft time by DM once the board fills.

  • Discord required. All draft-night communication and pick clocks run through the league server.
  • In-game or Showdown username. Used to verify Regulation M-B legality on your roster.
  • One roster per trainer. No proxy drafting or holding a slot for a friend.
  • Show up on draft night. Miss your pick window twice and your slot passes to the waitlist.
